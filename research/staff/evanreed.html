Asst. Prof. Evan Reed
Principal Investigator
evanreed _at_ stanford.edu

Education

  • Ph.D. in Physics, MIT, 2003
  • B.S. in Applied Physics, Caltech, 1998
Biography

Evan Reed is an Assistant Professor in Stanford's Department of Materials Science and Engineering since December 2009. His PhD was done with John Joannopoulos at MIT on shock wave compression of materials and photonic crystals.

Prior to Stanford, he spent 5 years at Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory as a staff scientist and E. O. Lawrence Fellow. His research develops atom-based and other materials modeling techniques aimed at energy applications, ultrafast science, and materials under extreme conditions.
