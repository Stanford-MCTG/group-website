Biography

Yuan Shen is currently a graduate student in the Physics Department at Stanford University.

Yuan received his Bachelor's degree in Physics From Fudan University where he also finished all compulsory courses for math majors. His interest is spread widely in theoretical condensed matter physics. In his undergraduate institute, he worked on liquid-solid-liquid contact angles, chirality switching with transformation optics and graphene plasmonics.

Yuan became a rotation student in Evan Reed's group from the winter of 2012, and now he is attempting to develop theoretical and computational methods for crystals under finite time-dependent electric fields.

Soccer and badminton are Yuan's favorite sports for his leisure time. He also loves playing video games and assembling/disassembling electronic devices.
