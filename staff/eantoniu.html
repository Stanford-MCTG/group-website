Education

Biography
Evan is a PhD student in the Department of Chemistry. He joined the Reed group in the Winter quarter of 2017. Currently, Evan is using DFT to investigate the properties of new Van der Waals heterostructures.

Evan received a B.Sc. in Chemistry from the University of Alberta in the Spring of 2015. While there, he performed research on a number of topics including the study of the catalytic hydrogenation of amides with Prof. Steven Bergens and using density functional theory to study the stability of low-oxidation state main group element hybrides with Prof. Alex Brown.

Evan enjoys biking, playing soccer, and trying new foods and restaurants.
