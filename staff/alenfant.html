Education

  • M.S., Engineering in Computer Science ("Diplôme d'ingénieur") specialized in Software Development, Institut Supérieur d'Electronique de Paris (ISEP), 2013 (expected)
Biography

Antonin spent two years in preparatory cycle at ISEP, consisting mainly in Mathematics and Physics studies.

After this, he continued his studies in the engineering cycle at ISEP for a total of three years where he took Computer Science courses and specialized in Software Development. During this time, he worked as a consultant for Junior ISEP, a small non-profit organization ran by the school who offered development services and as an intern for six months in Smart Impulse, a small startup in the energy sector.

Antonin joined Evan Reed's group as a visiting researcher during the winter of 2013. His work focuses on data mining and finding ways to apply Computer Science to solve problems in Materials Science.

His hobbies are reading books, watching movies, personal development projects and learning new things, but also biking and visiting the Bay Area.
