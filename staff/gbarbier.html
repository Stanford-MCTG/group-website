Education

  • M.S., Engineering in Computer Science ("Diplôme d'ingénieur"), ISEP, 2012
Biography

Guillaume spent his first two years studying Mathematics and Physics at ISEP. Then, he entered the engineering school and worked especially on Computer Science. He did business as a project manager and a consultant in a junior enterprise known as Junior ISEP. He also worked as a research intern for several months in the Bell Labs in France on various projects related to Software Engineering.

Guillaume came as a visiting researcher in Evan Reed's group in the winter of 2012. He is focusing on Neural Networks and their applications in Materials Science as a scalable computational solution.

His favorite sports are aikido, swimming and badminton. He also enjoys playing violin, hiking and programming.
