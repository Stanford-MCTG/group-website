Shai Barak
Graduate Student - Department of Electrical Engineering
sbarak _at_ stanford.edu

Education

  • M.S. Electrical Engineering, Stanford University, 2008
  • B.S. Electrical Engineering, Caltech, 2007
Biography

Shai received her B.S. in Electrical Engineering from the California Institute of Technology (Caltech) in 2007 and her M.S. in Electrical Engineering from Stanford in 2009. Her interests include the interactions between optics and materials, specificially laser-material interactions.

She is currently working on examining the interactions between femtosecond laser pulses and glass, working with Prof. Alberto Salleo on the experimental side and Prof. Evan Reed on the computational side. In her spare time, she also enjoys reading, papercraft, drawing, and cooking with friends.
