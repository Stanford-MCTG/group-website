Biography
Doğuş uses a combination of quantum mechanical calculations and machine learning tools to study and design materials. Applications so far include disordered solids, glassy liquids, Li-ion batteries, and phase change materials.

In his free time, he enjoys playing soccer, volleyball, basketball, and spikeball.
