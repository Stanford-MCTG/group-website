Biography
Ilkyu is currently a Ph.D. student in the Applied Physics department at Stanford University. He received his Bachelor's in Physics at Columbia University.

Ilkyu's research focuses on studying electronic long-range interactions in the time-dependent density functional theory framework and its applications to 2D materials.

In his free time, he likes to dance and dabble in parkour.
